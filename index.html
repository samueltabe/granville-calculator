Notes: This tool assumes revenue is limited by PV generation. PCS/Battery/container cost is entered as a single all-in value and combined with PV CAPEX to derive total hardware cost. BOS CAPEX scales as a percentage of hardware CAPEX. Annual O&M is a fixed percentage of total CAPEX. Annual energy sold in Year 1 is reduced over time using the Annual Battery Degradation input; project-level payback and ROI are computed over a 20-year life. The financing layer assumes a level-payment amortising loan; equity cashflows are calculated after debt service and used to derive equity payback, equity ROI and minimum DSCR during the loan tenor (screening-level only).
